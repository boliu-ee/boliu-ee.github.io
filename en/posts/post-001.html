Building a Bilingual Personal Website

Published: 2026-03-16 · Paired Chinese article available
中文版文章 →

This is a sample English article that demonstrates page-level bilingual linking. For every Chinese post, you can create a corresponding English version with the same topic and connect them directly.

Recommended Practice

  • Use the same post ID in both languages, such as post-001.html.
  • Place a visible language switch at the top of each article page.
  • If one version is not ready yet, keep a placeholder page and complete it later.

Good Content for Your Site

You can publish technical blogs, project summaries, paper notes, experiment logs, and tool tutorials here, then decide whether to write Chinese or English first based on the audience.
